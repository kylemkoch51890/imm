Our Capabilities

IMM is a shop that is built around helping you with your full project scope. We can assist you in everything regarding design, production, fabrication, molds and so much more.

Manufacturing and Design

Your products will be manufactured according to your specifications directly from prints or CAD files. With a full team of experienced machinists and engineers, we can also help you develop your product. Through design and testing, we’ll manufacture exactly what you need.

Engineering

Replacement parts that are no longer made can be reverse engineered easily. Our team will study the parts, build drawings and manufacture the part for you. At the end of the job, we will also provide you with a copy of the drawings.

In–house Fabrication

In order to better serve our customers, we discovered there was a need for in–house fabrication. We also partner with local fabricators to get large–scale jobs completed quickly and cost–effectively.

Partnerships

Although IMM has a wide variety of machines, we partner with trusted, proven vendors to ensure you receive your completed project from just one source. This way you don’t have to move from shop to shop.

We can handle any job you bring to us. Whether it involves design, manufacturing and fabrication, or you need to rerun a part previously made, we’ve got you covered. If you are looking for high–quality, cost–effective parts, give us a call.
